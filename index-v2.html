FAQ

Common questions.

I can only train 3 days a week. Will this work?+

Yes. There is a 3-day full body option designed for exactly that.

Do I need any special equipment?+

A commercial gym with barbells, dumbbells, cables, and basic machines. No specialty equipment.

How long are the workouts?+

45 to 75 minutes depending on the day.

What if I am a beginner?+

This program is built for people with training experience. If you have been lifting consistently for at least 6 months, you will be fine.

Can I do this alongside another sport?+

Yes. The 3-day option works well alongside other training. The 4-day option is more demanding but manageable with proper recovery.

When does new programming drop?+

Every week. It builds on the previous week so your progress compounds over time.
